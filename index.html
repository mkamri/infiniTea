about

all-natural

We use all-natural ingredients from organic, fair-trade farms around the world. We never use dyes, artificial flavors, or preservatives.

customizable

We hand-select and blend three teas every month for you to sample and enjoy. This means you can customize your box to get more of your favorites.

free shipping

We offer fast and free shipping to anyone in the US and Canada! Your infiniTea box will be delivered by the 5th of each month, guaranteed.

our process

fair-trade hand-picked tea for all.

We source our teas from fair-trade farms across the world. First we dry the tea leaves using an all-natural aging process. After that, we add our dried fruits, herbs, or natural flavors. Finally we hand-package and ship our teas directly to you in a 100% post-consumer recycled package. Enjoy tea the natural way!
